---
description: Comprehensive crypto project research covering utility, tokenomics, revenue, and sentiment.
---

1.  **Initial Discovery & Link Gathering**
    -   **Goal**: Find the official entry points for the project.
    -   **Action**: Search the web for `[ProjectName/Symbol] CoinMarketCap` and `[ProjectName/Symbol] Gate.io`.
    -   **Extract**:
        -   Official Website
        -   Whitepaper (Docs)
        -   Block Explorer (Etherscan, Solscan, etc.)
        -   Twitter/X handle
        -   Total Supply & Circulating Supply (from specific pages).

2.  **Fundamental Analysis (What & How)**
    -   **Goal**: Understand the core business and token role.
    -   **Action**: Use `read_url_content` (or `browser`) on the Website and Whitepaper.
    -   **Answer Questions**:
        -   **Project Overview**: What is the project? What problem does it solve? (赛道/简述)
        -   **Chain**: Which blockchain is it on? (Check Explorer or Docs)
        -   **Revenue**: Does the protocol generate revenue? How? (Fees, Treasury, etc.)
        -   **Token Utility**: What is the token used for? (Governance, Gas, Staking, Payment?)

3.  **Tokenomics & Funding Deep Dive**
    -   **Goal**: Analyze the financial structure.
    -   **Action**: Search specifically for:
        -   `[ProjectName] tokenomics allocation distribution`
        -   `[ProjectName] vesting schedule unlock`
        -   `[ProjectName] investors funding rounds seed`
    -   **Answer Questions**:
        -   **Allocation**: Percentages for Team, Ecosystem, Airdrop, Investors.
        -   **Circulation & Unlocks**: Current circulation, Total supply, Vesting terms (Cliff/Linear).
        -   **Funding**: Who invested? (VC names) How much raised? Valuation?

4.  **On-Chain & Sentiment Check**
    -   **Goal**: Verify activity and social mood.
    -   **Action**: 
        -   Check the Explorer link found in Step 1 for "Holders" to see distribution concentration.
        -   Search web/Twitter for: `[ProjectName] crypto twitter sentiment`, `[ProjectName] partnership`, `[ProjectName] recent news`.
    -   **Answer Questions**:
        -   **Social Discussion**: What is the community discussing? (Bullish/Bearish/FUD)
        -   **Recent Events**: Any recent hacks, upgrades, or listings?

5.  **Final Report Generation**
    -   **Constraint**: Use the exact format requested below.
    -   **Format**:
        ```markdown
        # [Project Name] ([Symbol]) 投研报告

        ## 1. 项目是做什么的？(Project Overview)
        [Content]

        ## 2. 是哪条链上的？(Chain)
        [Content]

        ## 3. 有没有收入？(Revenue Model)
        [Content]

        ## 4. 代币的作用是什么？(Token Utility)
        [Content]

        ## 5. 目前的流通量和解锁情况是什么？(Tokenomics)
        - **总量/流通量**: ...
        - **解锁规则**: ...

        ## 6. 代币分配比例如何？(Allocation)
        [Content]

        ## 7. 推特上关于它的讨论是什么？近期有什么新闻或者重大事件吗？(Social & News)
        [Content]

        ## 8. 融资的机构有哪些，分别融了多少钱？(Funding)
        [Content]
        ```
